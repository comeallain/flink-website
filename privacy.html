Information We Collect

When you use our Platform, we may collect two types of information:

1. Personal Information

This is information that identifies or can be used to identify you. This includes:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and postal address (e.g., when you create an account or contact us).
  • Payment details (payment processor handles this securely; we don’t store full credit/debit card information).
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide, such as when filling out forms or communicating with our support team.

2. Non-Personal Information

This is data that does not directly identify you and may include:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system.
  • IP address, location data, and browsing behavior.
  • Analytics data such as how you interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, session duration).

How We Collect Your Information

We gather information using the following methods:

  • Direct Input: When you create an account, subscribe to a newsletter, engage with customer support, or share your information directly.
  • Automated Technologies: Cookies, tracking pixels, and similar technologies collect information about your interactions with our Platform. For more details, see our Cookies Policy below.
  • Third-Party Sources: We may receive information from third-party vendors such as payment processors, analytics providers, or marketing platforms.

How We Use Your Information

1. Provide and Enhance the Service

  • Enable account creation, film distribution, and user interactions.
  • Process transactions and ensure secure payment.
  • Offer a more personalized experience (e.g., language preferences).

2. Communication

  • Send transactional emails such as payment receipts or account updates.
  • Share company news, updates, or promotional material (with your consent).

3. Validation and Security

  • Verify your identity or legal ownership of uploaded content.

4. Compliance and Legal Obligations

  • Fulfill regulatory compliance, including copyright laws and KYC (Know Your Customer) processes.

5. Analytics and Improvements

  • Monitor performance and fix bugs or technical issues.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following scenarios:

1. With Service Providers

We work with trusted third parties to:

  • Provide analytics or customer service support.

We may share information if required by law or to protect our legal rights, comply with regulations, or prevent fraud or other unlawful activities.

You have certain rights to control your personal data, including:

Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may impact the functionality of our Platform.
